Creating Partnerships of Purpose

"There is no one who loves pain itself, who seeks after it and wants to have it, simply because it is pain..."


Yayasan Kasih Karunia Yudany, literally meaning Yudany Love and Grace Foundation ("Yudany"), is a non-profit organization. It was established as a manifestation of love and concern for children who need special attentions, as a result of economic difficulties, psycho-social and physiobiological problems. Children are the fruits of grace given by the Creator for the mankind, and the future of the world lies in their hands. Their happy smile is the reflection of the sun for the brightness of our life. Yudany presents as a light and hope for those who have lost their way and faith of life in the midst of an increasingly egocentric world. Inspired and founded based on the essence of love and grace, Yudany is trying to give a more meaningful life to children with special needs through psycho-social support, education, and physiotherapy.


One of the media set up to bring in such support is through Yayasan Heesu "Cahaya Cinta" or Heesu "Light of Love" Foundation which was established since 2008. Heesu "Cahaya Cinta" Foundation ("Heesu") is focusing on care and empowerment of children with multiple disabilities, in order for them to become more independent, both physically and socially. Backed by professionals in their field with a spirit of sincere love, Heesu keeps growing and has been able to empower 19 children from a variety of medical and social background until now. Heesu's existence has been a channel of blessings for children and their families, including for the neighborhoods where Heesu is located.


We believe that as long as love exists, the same spirit for us to be a channel of grace for children with special needs will continue burning.
